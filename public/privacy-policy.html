Privacy policy

This policy applies only to activities MobiMatter LTD engages in on its website MobiMatter.com and MobiMatter Smartphone app (“Website”). It does not apply to MobiMatter activities that are “offline” or unrelated to the website or the app.

The Website collects certain anonymous data regarding the usage of the website. This information does not personally identify users by itself. The anonymous data collected by the Website can include information such as the type of browser you are using, and the length of the visit to the Website and the App. You may also be asked to provide personally identifiable information, which may include your name, address, telephone number and e-mail address. This information can be gathered when feedback or e-mails are sent to the Website, when you register for services, or make purchases via the website. In all such cases, you have the option of providing us with personally identifiable information.

All credit/debit cards’ details will NOT be stored, sold, shared, rented or leased to any third parties.

The Website may use non-personal “aggregated data” to enhance the operation of our website or analyze interest in the areas of our website. Additionally, if you provide MobiMatter.com with content for publishing or feedback, we may publish your username or other identifying data.

PARTNER SERVICE PROVIDERS. MobiMatter LTD is an online agent selling services provided by its partners (telecom operators, service aggregators, etc.). MobiMatter abides by the data and KYC document collection policy of its partners, who operate in different jurisdictions. Prior to any service purchase, the Website will provide a link to the relevant partner service provider’s T&C’s and privacy policies. We strongly suggest the users to review such T&Cs and privacy policies. The website user will be asked to confirm its consent to prior to proceeding with the purchase. Any such personal data obtained from the Website user on behalf of the MobiMatter partner will be shared with the partner. MobiMatter LTD is not responsible for the privacy policies of its partner telecom service providers whose services it sells. Any information obtained by such third parties can be subject to different rules regarding the collection and use of your personal information, which is beyond the control and responsibility of MobiMatter LTD. The Website users should contact these entities directly if they have any questions about their use of the information that they collect.

MobiMatter LTD may also provide such personally identifiable information in response to a law enforcement agency request or as otherwise required by law. Your personally identifiable information may be provided to a party if MobiMatter LTD files for bankruptcy, or there is a transfer of the assets or ownership of the Website in connection with proposed or consummated corporate reorganizations, such as mergers or acquisitions.

SECURITY. MobiMatter LTD takes appropriate steps to ensure data privacy and security including through various hardware and software methodologies. However, MobiMatter LTD cannot guarantee the security of any information that is disclosed online.

MINORS. The Website does not knowingly collect personal information from minors under the age of 18. Minors are not permitted to use the Website or services, and MobiMatter LTD requests that minors under the age of 18 not submit any personal information to the website. Since information regarding minors under the age of 18 is not collected, MobiMatter LTD does not knowingly distribute personal information regarding minors under the age of 18.
